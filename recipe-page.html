How to Make a Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread 🍞
  • 1 tbsp butter 🧈
  • Cheese slice 🧀
  • Lettuce & Tomato 🥬🍅
  • Optional: Ham or Veggies

Steps:

  1. Spread butter on one side of the bread.
  2. Add cheese, lettuce, tomato, and optional fillings.
  3. Cover with the second slice of bread.
  4. Cut diagonally and serve with love ❤️
